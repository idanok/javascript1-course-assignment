OUR STORY

We are a group of good friends who always wanted to make good and sustainable jackets for our outdoor adventures. We share a lot of interests, such as: hiking, rafting, skiing, camping, canoeing and exploring nature. Outdoor clothing is therefor very important to us and we are passionate about sharing our products with the world for a better outdoor experience. The environment is something we all really cares about and we always had a goal to make the jackets as sustainable as possible.

OUR FOCUS

Fast fashion is growing faster then ever and comes with an astonishing environmental and social cost. We in Rainydays are therefor committed to making the jackets as sustainable as possible. The material is made out of recycled materials and uses biodegradable components from recycled fibers. These materials grow with no pesticide or fertilizer, consume less energy and water, and employ no chemical treatment, thus reducing the overall carbon footprint. We also use fabrics that specializes in sustainable clothes. These fabrics are also local so that we can meet with the producer without having to travel by plane. We are determent to do what we can for a more sustainable world.
